Your Rising Sign (Ascendant)

What's a Rising Sign, Anyhow?

In astrology, your rising sign is the portal through which you perceive the external world. Your rising sign, or ascendant, governs elements of your appearance as well as the manifestation of your outward personality. It is also the first impression you give to others. Think of it as your facade, the natural mask to which you default when you're with unfamiliar people, in unfamiliar places and situations. When you walk into a room, you are radiating the qualities of your rising sign. If you are a Leo sun with a Scorpio rising, then although you are a Leo at heart and people will get to know that lion or lioness within, when people meet you for the first time, they meet a Scorpio. The presentation of the personality, as determined by the ascendant, always precedes the presentation of one's true essence -- what one is at one's core, what is at the center of your being.

Your rising sign is determined by your birth date, location, and your accurate birth time, as it changes zodiac signs approximately every two hours. Technically speaking, it's the zodiac sign that was rising or ascending on the eastern horizon the moment you were born. The rising sign sets up the pie-shaped structure of the 12 houses in your birth chart and informs your chart ruler.

Essentially, your rising sign is your public persona, the "hat" or "costume" you wear when people first meet you. It is how you initially appear to others, the first impression that you make. In some ways, it is a facade, for it is not who we truly are deep within ourselves, at our core -- the nature of our real, true essence is described by our Sun sign -- but rather, the ascendant sign describes how we present ourselves to the external world and how we wish to be seen.

If Your Rising Sign is...

ARIES

Symbol: The Ram

You were born with the first sign of the zodiac on your ascendant, making Mars your ruling planet and an important part of your natal chart. Yours is considered the "natural" chart. Even if your Sun sign is in an earth, air, or water element, you come across as pure fire due to your Aries rising qualities. Your personality conveys drive, action, raw sexuality, impulsivity, and confident zeal. You have a natural assertiveness and passionate air about you. Aries is a cardinal fire sign symbolized by the sturdy Ram, giving you a pioneering spirit. You are often seen as a trailblazer and someone who is unafraid to take risks. You are known for your boundless enthusiasm and competitive nature. Famous Aries risings include Penélope Cruz, Kendall Jenner, Barbra Streisand, Shakira, Joan Rivers, and Rihanna. Oh, and also the author of the content of this site, Elizabeth Kennedy.

TAURUS

Symbol: The Bull

Taurus rising natives are often rather stubborn and fixed in their ways. If you are a Taurus rising, then it can be quite difficult to sway you. By nature, you are resistant to change. You possess a determined single-mindedness. Security is one of your foremost considerations before undertaking anything new. You have a well-developed sense of smell and touch, and respond to the material world. Yours is a practical approach to life. A lover of luxury and all of the finer things, you place a lot of value on your material possessions. Taurus rising natives are often collectors of some kind. You have a stability about you that is soothing and an inner harmony that is attractive, so you are very comforting to be around. You prefer to wear comfortable clothes that feel good against your skin. You are likely to be possessive in your personal relationships. Loyalty is very important to you. Famous Taurus risings include Queen Latifah, George Lucas, Liza Minnelli, Nick Nolte, Billy Crystal, Mia Farrow, Bo Derek, Carlos Santana, Sigourney Weaver, Serena Williams.

GEMINI

Symbol: The Twins

You have an innate ability to communicate effectively and connect with almost anyone. Mercury, the closest planet to the Sun and the ruler of communication, writing, speech, and intellect, is also the ruler of your chart. You are endowed with a warm demeanor, natural inquisitiveness, versatility, and a sharp intellect. You are not one for emotional displays and tend to use logic to navigate your reality. You are a social butterfly who loves new environments, exchanging ideas, and a variety of information. You are childlike, funny, and playful. People are drawn to your charming disposition and curiosity. You are adaptable, which sometimes translates into being indecisive or a "fence-sitter." You easily switch between different personas, and you can be plagued by a sense of restlessness. Famous Gemini risings include Lady Gaga, Sarah Jessica Parker, LeBron James, Kristen Stewart, Willow Smith, Sandra Bullock, Lindsay Lohan, Dua Lipa, and Drew Barrymore.

CANCER

Symbol: The Crab

You can be nurturing, motivated by security, and focused on protecting yourself. Cancer is a cardinal water sign, and the Moon is the ruler of your chart. You can come across to others as compassionate, empathetic, and in touch with your feelings. You can also be quite moody and reactive, and easily affected by those around you. This is especially true if you haven't gained the wisdom needed to detach and distinguish your own feelings from others. Sometimes others perceive you as being reserved, shy, or cautious. Famous Cancer risings include Angelina Jolie, Julia Roberts, Cameron Diaz, Adele, The Weeknd, Ben Affleck, Victoria Beckham, Meghan Markle, Margot Robbie, Tyra Banks, Cindy Crawford, and Pharrell Williams.

LEO

Symbol: The Lion

Drama comes naturally to you. Leo rising people cannot help but be noticed. You radiate a special energy and have a natural magnetism that gets others' attention. You are very self-aware and body-conscious. You might be prone to rash decision-making, temper tantrums, and general excess. However, you also have plenty of staying power and drive, and your idealism keeps things light and also helps to keep you from getting into too much trouble. You have a strong authoritarian manner and a youthful appearance, regardless of your actual age. You have a natural flair for presentation, an eye for quality, and a hard-to-resist warmth of style. You are warm, fun-loving, often childlike in an endearing way, generous and spirited. You have an unusual need to be admired. Famous Leo risings include Justin Timberlake, Drake, Kirsten Dunst, Matthew Perry, Vanessa Hudgens, Blake Lively, George Bush Jr., Muhammed Ali, Jessica Lange, Frida Kahlo, Meryl Streep, Donald Trump, Elton John, Celine Dion, Jake Gyllenhaal, Marilyn Monroe.

VIRGO

Symbol: The Virgin

There is a quiet charm to many Virgo rising people. You have an intelligent and reserved aura that is unmistakable. You might be exceptionally particular about food. Even if your appetite is generally good, you are likely to be very picky about what you put into your body. You are probably quite focused on physical health and might take a special interest in the mind-body connection. You notice the tiniest details often overlooked by others. You may have a tendency to worry a lot, especially when confronted with new people and situations. You tend to appear rather collected and professional. There is often something scholarly in your appearance; your natural demeanor may carry an air of academia in some way. Others perceive you as being highly intellectually gifted. Regardless of your actual profession, you look like a teacher, an author, a scholar, or otherwise someone who is a genius in their chosen field. Famous Virgo risings include Emma Watson, W. LIBRA

Symbol: The Scales

Libra Rising people don't know what to do with themselves without a significant other. If you are a Libra rising native, you can be enormously persuasive, although you will almost always use a “soft sell” approach when you want to win others over, which is all of the time! A tendency to pass the buck and keep up that “nice guy/gal” image can be amongst your worst qualities. However, you can make excellent mediators, when you want to, and will generally be the first to accommodate others, or at least give the appearance of doing so. Libra rising natives are usually attracted to competent, active partners. Their relationships are often characterized by bickering or competitiveness until they learn to drop their sweet image once in a while and to stop blaming their partners for everything that goes wrong. It's certainly a pretty facade, but don't make the mistake of underestimating the subtle cruel streak simmering just beneath the surface of things. Famous Libra risings include Britney Spears, Denzel Washington, Harrison Ford, Jennifer Aniston, Anne Heche, Leonardo DiCaprio, Jean-Claude Van Damme, Freddie Prinze Jr., Kendrick Lamar, Gerard Butler.
