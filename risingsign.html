Your Rising Sign (Ascendant)

What's a Rising Sign, Anyhow?

Your rising sign is determined by your birth date, location, and your accurate birth time, as it changes zodiac signs approximately every two hours. Technically speaking, it's the zodiac sign that was rising or ascending on the eastern horizon the moment you were born. The rising sign sets up the pie-shaped structure of the 12 houses in your birth chart and informs your chart ruler.

Essentially, your rising sign is your public persona, the "hat" or "costume" you wear when people first meet you. It is how you initially appear to others, the first impression that you make. In some ways, it is a facade, for it is not who we truly are deep within ourselves, at our core -- the nature of our real, true essence is described by our Sun sign -- but rather, the ascendant sign describes how we present ourselves to the external world and how we wish to be seen.

Your personality conveys drive, action, raw sexuality, impulsivity, and confident zeal. You have a natural assertiveness and passionate air about you. Aries is a cardinal fire sign symbolized by the sturdy Ram, giving you a pioneering spirit. You are often seen as a trailblazer and someone who is unafraid to take risks. GEMINI

Symbol: The Twins

You have an innate ability to communicate effectively and connect with almost anyone. Mercury, the closest planet to the Sun and the ruler of communication, writing, speech, and intellect, is also the ruler of your chart. You are endowed with a warm demeanor, natural inquisitiveness, versatility, and a sharp intellect. You are not one for emotional displays and tend to use logic to navigate your reality. People are drawn to your charming disposition and curiosity. You are adaptable, which sometimes translates into being indecisive or a "fence-sitter." You easily switch between different personas, and you can be plagued by a sense of restlessness. CANCER

Symbol: The Crab

You can be nurturing, motivated by security, and focused on protecting yourself. Cancer is a cardinal water sign, and the Moon is the ruler of your chart. You can come across to others as compassionate, empathetic, and in touch with your feelings. You can also be quite moody and reactive, and easily affected by those around you. This is especially true if you haven't gained the wisdom needed to detach and distinguish your own feelings from others. Sometimes others perceive you as being reserved, shy, or cautious. Famous Cancer risings include Angelina Jolie, Julia Roberts, Cameron Diaz, Adele, The Weeknd, Ben Affleck, Victoria Beckham, Meghan Markle, Margot Robbie, Tyra Banks, Cindy Crawford, and Pharrell Williams.
