Your Moon Sign

How you relate to others, your feelings and emotions

If Your Moon is in...

ARIES: Aries moons are said to be emotionally intense and impulsive, and to feel and express emotions quickly. They are said to be emotionally satisfied by excitement, spontaneity, and victory.

TAURUS: Taurus moons are said to be emotionally stable and deep, and they store their feelings deeply. They can be slow to adapt to change and may hold onto grudges.

GEMINI: Gemini moon people have feelings that can be as restless as their thoughts, especially when life gets challenging or they need to make important decisions.

CANCER: Cancer moon people are said to be deeply emotional, sensitive, and intuitive. They may be in touch with their emotions to a degree that some may say is excessive.

LEO: A Leo moon sign is said to indicate a personality that is bold, bright, and magnetic, with a love of attention and a natural curiosity.

VIRGO: Virgo Moons are renowned for their unwavering loyalty and devotion in their relationships.

LIBRA: People with a Libra moon sign are natural mediators who thrive in partnerships and are happiest when keeping the peace. They are also said to be charming, graceful, and elegant, and to get along well with many people.

SCORPIO: People with a Scorpio Moon take their relationships seriously and want to feel a sense of strong, long-lasting relationship with partners. They have a special ability to display how they feel and combine emotion with desire quickly so that they can communicate on an emotional level.

SAGITTARIUS: Sagittarius Moon individuals are truth-seekers, always in pursuit of the ultimate truth, whether in personal interactions or in understanding the world at large. You value transparency and straightforwardness, both in yourself and in others, and are often considered a trustworthy and reliable confidant.

CAPRICORN: Individuals with a Capricorn moon tend to exhibit certain characteristics such as emotional reserve, ambition, and a strong sense of responsibility. They often approach their emotions in a practical and disciplined manner, prioritizing stability and long-term goals.

AQUARIUS: Aquarius moon individuals find happiness in freedom, creativity, and making a positive difference in the world. They thrive in environments where they can express themselves freely, explore new ideas, and contribute to societal progress.

PISCES: Pisces Moon individuals are the oceanic souls of the zodiac. Their emotional depth is unmatched, and they possess a natural ability to empathize with others. This empathy stems from their intuitive understanding of the human condition, making them exceptional listeners and friends.
